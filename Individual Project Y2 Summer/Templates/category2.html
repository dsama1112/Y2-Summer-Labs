Condensed Milk Truffles Recipe

Here is the recipe for the Condensed Milk Truffles:

  • Ingredients: 395g can NESTLÉ Sweetened Condensed Milk 30g (1/4 cup) NESTLÉ Plaistowe Premium Dutch Process Cocoa, plus extra to dust
  • Directions: Place the condensed milk and cocoa in a medium saucepan over low heat. Cook, stirring, for 8 minutes or until mixture is very thick. Transfer mixture to a bowl. Set aside to cool slightly before placing in the fridge for 1 hour to set. Use two teaspoonsful of the mixture to roll into smooth balls. Dust in extra cocoa powder to serve.

Homemade ‘Bounty’ Bars

Here is the recipe for how to make homemade ‘Bounty’ bars:

  • Ingredients: 340g (4 cups) desiccated coconut, 395g can sweetened condensed milk, 290g pkt milk chocolate melts
  • Directions: Line a baking tray with baking paper. Place coconut and sweetened condensed milk in a large bowl and stir until well combined. Shape mixture into 5cm logs. Transfer to prepared tray. Place in the fridge until firm. Place chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on High for 1 minute, stirring halfway, until melted and smooth. Using two forks, dip each each coconut bar in chocolate until coated, allowing excess to drip back into bowl. Return to tray and place in fridge until set. Serve.

Microwave Orea Mug Cake

Here is the recipe for how to make oreo mug cake by microwave:

  • Ingredients: 8 oreos, 3 tablespoons whole milk
  • Directions: Using a microwavable mug, smash the oreos into pieces then mix with required amount of milk. Place mug in the microwave for 1.5-3 minutes till ready!
