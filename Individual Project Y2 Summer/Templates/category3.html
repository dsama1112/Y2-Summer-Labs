Strawberry and Dark Chocolate Drizzle Cheesecake Bites

Here is the recipe for the Strawberry and Dark Chocolate Drizzle Cheesecake Bites:

  • Ingredients: 250g cream cheese, softened, 1/4 cup icing sugar mixture, 1 tsp vanilla bean paste, 19 Oreo cookies, 150g Coles dark chocolate, melted, 8 small strawberries, halved
  • Directions: Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla for 1 minute or until smooth. Place cream cheese mixture in a snap-lock bag. Snip off 1 corner. Pipe mixture onto 16 cookies. Finely chop remaining cookies. Drizzle cheesecakes with chocolate and sprinkle with chopped cookies. Top with strawberries. Serve.

Keto Lemon Mug Dessert Cake

Here is the recipe for how to make Keto Lemon Mug Dessert Cake:

  • Ingredients: 20g butter, melted, cooled, 1 egg, 1 tsp finely grated lemon rind, plus extra zested, to serve (optional), 25g (1/4 cup) coconut flour, 1 tbsp ground almonds, 2 tsp xylitol, 1/2 tsp baking powder, 2 tbsp almond milk, 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice. Mascarpone, to serve
  • Directions: Whisk together the butter, egg and lemon rind in a small mixing bowl until combined. Add the flour, almond, xylitol, baking powder, milk and juice. Stir to combine. Transfer mixture to a 250ml (1 cup) heatproof mug or cup and smooth the surface. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes. Set aside for 30 seconds. Top with a dollop of mascarpone and extra lemon zest, if using, to serve.

Chocolate Chip Cookies with Choc-Marshmallow Dip

Here is the recipe for how to make Chocolate Chip Cookies with Choc-Marshmallow Dip:

  • Ingredients: 75g Coles Brand Belgian Milk Chocolate, 13 white marshmallows, 400g pkt Coles Brand Chocolate Chip Cookies
  • Directions: Preheat oven to 220C or 200C fan-forced. Finely chop Coles Brand Belgian Milk Chocolate. Place in a 1 1/4-cup ovenproof dish. Top with 13 white marshmallows, flat-side up. Bake for 4-5 minutes or until the chocolate melts and marshmallows become golden. Stand for 5 minutes. Serve with Coles Brand Chocolate Chip Cookies for dipping.
